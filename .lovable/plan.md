

# Goldn Enterprises — Service Business Website

## Overview
A modern, mobile-first, sky blue & white themed website for Goldn Enterprises (Founder: Sachin Kumar Sahu) — an AC & home appliance repair business. Includes a service area map section.

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Logo/company name "Goldn Enterprises"
- Links to each section (Services, About, Why Us, Contact)
- "Call Now" button in header
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width background image (AC technician at work via Unsplash)
- Heading: "Fast & Reliable AC & Home Appliance Repair Services"
- Subheading: "Expert technicians at your doorstep"
- Two CTA buttons: **Call Now** (tel:9310912781) and **Book Service** (scrolls to contact form)
- Fade-in animation on load

### 3. Services Section
- 6 service cards in a responsive grid (1 col mobile, 2 col tablet, 3 col desktop)
- Each card: relevant Unsplash image, Lucide icon, title, short real description
- Services: AC Repair & Installation, Refrigerator Repair, Washing Machine Repair, Air Cooler Repair, Water Dispenser Repair, All Home Electronics Repair
- Hover scale animation on cards

### 4. About Section
- Founder spotlight: Sachin Kumar Sahu
- Trust-building content: years of experience, reliability, doorstep service promise
- Two-column layout (text + image)

### 5. Why Choose Us
- 6 feature points with icons: Same Day Service, Affordable Pricing, Expert Technicians, Trusted Service, Quick Response, Doorstep Service
- Grid layout with fade-in animations

### 6. Service Area Map
- Embedded Google Maps iframe showing Delhi/NCR region (primary service area)
- List of areas served (e.g., Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, Ghaziabad)
- Two-column layout: map on one side, area list on the other
- Clean cards or badges for each service area

### 7. CTA Banner
- Bold sky-blue background
- Text: "Need urgent repair? Call now!"
- Large "Call Now" button

### 8. Contact Section
- Two-column: contact form + contact info
- Form fields: Name, Phone, Service (dropdown)
- Clickable phone number
- Address placeholder

### 9. Footer
- Company name, phone, copyright, quick links

## Floating Elements
- **Sticky Call Button** (mobile only): fixed bottom bar with "Call Now"
- **WhatsApp Button**: floating green button linking to wa.me/919310912781

## Design
- **Colors**: Sky blue (#0ea5e9) and white with dark text
- **Mobile-first**: fully responsive
- **Animations**: fade-in on scroll, hover-scale on cards
- Reusable components, proper heading hierarchy, optimized images
